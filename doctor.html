Head Consultant


Dr. Ashwini Malhotra


PULMONOLOGIST


Dr. Ashwini Malhotra is a well-known pulmonologist in Bhopal, currently practicing at Bansal Hospital. For the past 16 years, Dr. Malhotra has worked as a lung specialist and gained adequate knowledge and skills in the field. He received his MBBS degree from Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Medical College, Jabalpur. He did a fellowship in intensive care medicine(F.I.C.M.) from Apollo Hospital, New Delhi. He also holds a Diploma in TB and Chest medicine from M.G.M Medical College, Indore, and a degree in D.N.B.(Pulmonary Medicine) from Apollo Hospital, New Delhi. Prior to his position as a pulmonologist at Bansal Hospital, he worked as a senior consultant (pulmonology) at RBTB Hospital, Sri Aurobindo Institute of Medical Sciences, and Mahatma Gandhi Medical College in Indore, as well as an associate consultant (pulmonology) at Fortis Hospital in New Delhi.

Specialist of :Bronchoscopy , Pulmonary Rehabilitation

Consultant -- Reproductive Medicine (IVF)


Dr. Priya Bhave Chittawar


OBSTETRICOLOGIST


Dr. Priya Bhave Chittawar is one of the best and most trusted female gynecologists, IVF Specialist in Bhopal currently working as a Reproductive Medicine Specialist in Bansal Hospital, Bhopal. She achieved her MBBS degree from Gandhi Medical College and later went on to pursue M.D. (Obstetrics and Gynecology) from the same institute. She also holds a D.N.B. degree (Obstetrics and Gynecology) from National Board of Examination, New Delhi. Dr. Priya has expertise in Reproductive medicine and minimally invasive reproductive surgery. Reproductive medicine is a subspecialty that treats hormonal problems in both men and women, including subfertility. It uses assisted reproductive technologies like in vitro fertilization (IVF), intracytoplasmic sperm injection (ICSI), egg donation, and a variety of reproductive tract surgeries like laparoscopy and hysteroscopy. The Reproductive Medicine Unit of Bansal Hospital, Bhopal comprises the team of best gynecologists in M.P who are well equipped to deal with female-related disorders and has an in-house hormone assay facility. The department is committed to providing a holistic approach to dealing with patients with PCOS with diet therapy, exercise, and limited use of hormonal therapy. Regular workshops on diet and exercise therapy in patients with PCOS are an essential part of the treatment.

Specialist of :Obstetrics, Gynaecology & Reproductive Medicine
